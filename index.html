What is board wax?

Skate Wax Was Created To Make Obstacles Grindable

Board wax is used to enhance slides and grinds, allowing for a more smooth grind and a longer distance. Applied on the bottom of a board or on the obstacle itself, board wax is an easy way to enhance your experience. Skate wax allows even the most inexperienced rider to feel more engaged in the sport.

Why Glitch Wax?

A Simple Container With An Advanced Product
