How to Use It

Simple, familiar, and ready to ride.

Apply the wax directly to the bottom of your board or to the obstacle you plan to use. Add as much or as little as needed based on the surface and the feel you want. Store the pucks in a safe place between sessions to keep them clean and ready for the next use.
